# Information Security Risk Register

## Risk ID: R-001

**Risk Description**  
Unauthorized access to customer data due to weak access control practices.

**Threat**  
Credential theft through phishing or password reuse.

**Vulnerability**  
Lack of multi-factor authentication and infrequent access reviews.

**Business Impact**
- Regulatory penalties (GDPR)
- Loss of customer trust
- Contract termination by key clients

**Likelihood:** Medium  
**Impact:** High  
**Risk Level:** High

**Risk Owner:** Head of IT  
**Treatment Decision:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Measures**
- Enforce MFA for all users
- Quarterly access reviews
- Security awareness training

**Related Annex A Controls**
- A.5.15 Access control
- A.5.17 Authentication information
